myUCF

web design
2022

This project was a 10-week-long complete redesign of the University of Central Florida's student portal system, which I contributed to during my web design workshop course. This process required my team and I to complete a redeployment, testing, and refactoring phase. During this process, my main focus was to test various aspects of the original design, including accessibility, compatibility, and performance, to determine what problems needed to be solved, how, and why.
