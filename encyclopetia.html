encycloPETia

user-centered design project
spring 2021

view encycloPETia prototype

This project was a semester-long group production of the design phase of an app called encycloPETia, that I completed during my user-centered design course. This plan was built from thorough research and planning and consists of a site map, an evaluation, and critique of marketplace examples, defined users and stakeholders, market survey data, user goals, tasks and activities, use case scenario with think-aloud protocol, tasks analysis, interaction analysis, and design evaluation, a data dictionary, information architecture, navigation, search, sort, and retrieval results, low fidelity prototype, wireframe, and various high-fidelity designs.
